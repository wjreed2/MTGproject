Tips

  • Two modes: normal uses top+bottom strips, Set-only zoom focuses on footer set/# only
  • Set+number gives an exact match — far more reliable than reading the name font
  • Hold steady — OCR runs every ~3s, motion blurs the text
  • Good lighting, non-foil cards scan best; avoid glare on the bottom strip
  • Edit the name manually and hit Search if OCR misreads it
